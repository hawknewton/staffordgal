Stafford Gallery

  • Call
  • Email

Located on the plaza in the historic Plaza Arts Building in beautiful downtown Healdsburg, California.

Representing:

Leo E Osborne
Award-winning original wood burl and limited edition bronze wildlife sculpture by, as well as paintings in acrylic on gold leaf.

Craig Nelson
Impressionistic oil paintings of landscape, architecture, figurative works and still lifes set in Sonoma County, Europe and beyond.

Joan Dumouchel
Ethereal, figurative and theatrical dreamscapes by this French Canadian painter, inspired by Cirque du Soleil

William Crosby
Vibrant, abstract expressionist landscapes in acrylic

Michael Cone
Large-format abstract and earthscape photography in black and white limited editions

Mitch S Billis
Italian-inspired whimsical realist bronzes from maquette to monumental

Jennifer Spencer
Dramatic and sensitive still life compositions in oil

Glade Sarbach Davis
Original, handmade jewelry designs and resets by a master goldsmith

Nancy Linkin
Fluidly sculptural anticlastic jewelry designs in sterling and 18k by Nancy Linkin

Salatino-Gandolfo
Faceted, flowing and organic cold-worked glass forms by Salatino-Gandolfo Glass

Sidney Soriano
Exotic, lustrous pearl jewelry designs from the world over by Sidney Soriano
